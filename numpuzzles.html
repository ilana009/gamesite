Kakuro

Kakuro (addition cross) is one of the oldest grid logic puzzles in existence traced back to the April/May 1950 issue of Official Crossword Puzzles by Dell Publishing Company in the USA and refined by Monthly Nikolist magazine in Japan in 1986.

Numbers from 1 - 9 are inserted once into each row or column.

Sudoku

Sudoku (number only or number single) is a famous 9x9 number puzzle created by Swiss mathematician Leonhard Euler in 1782 and defined by Monthly Nikolist magazine in Japan in 1984.

Numbers from 1-9 are inserted once into each row and column
